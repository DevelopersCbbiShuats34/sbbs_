About the e-ConferenceThematic AreasGuidelines for registration

The Annual convention and National e-Conference on Agricultural, Veterinary and Biological Sciences is scheduled for December 21st-22nd, 2024, on a virtual platform. This conference aims to provide an outstanding National forum for scientists, academicians, researchers, engineers, industry professionals, and research scholars worldwide to present their research and engage with leading experts. The key objective is to facilitate the sharing of ideas, foster academic and research collaborations, and promote innovation in biotechnology, agriculture, veterinary, and biological sciences. The event will feature a wide array of invited talks from distinguished speakers, contributing to advancements in these vital scientific fields.
